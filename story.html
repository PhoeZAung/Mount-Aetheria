Dark Grasp: Session 1

Amidst of Dawn


Two unknown adventurers—Astra, a mischievous troublemaker, and Onyx, her reluctant companion—begin their journey on a quiet morning road. After sharing a meager meal at dusk, they're ambushed by a bandit, thrusting them into danger and the start of something far bigger than either expected. In combat, the duo defeated four bandits. The leader released an imp as a last resort, then fled the scene injured.

Shrine Lake
At sunset, the adventurers found a magical spring in an ancient crater. There, they met Cherrisa, a girl seeking to lift a curse by reaching Eor — the Golden Kingdom. After learning her story, they struck a deal: she’d feed them, and in return, they’d act as her bodyguards. Just then, Onyx heard a mysterious bell and followed it down the road.

Merchant Market
At the bustling market of Risha, filled with colorful stalls and guarded paths, the group wandered through, shopping for food and supplies until their coin ran dry. Before leaving, they slipped away with a beautiful white dress from a clothing stall — unnoticed and unbothered.
